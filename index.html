The House

A Dubai desk with a long memory.

Kanji General Trading is a privately held precious metals house operating from the DMCC Free Zone in Dubai. We do one thing, carefully — source, price and settle physical metals for a short list of institutional, sovereign and private clients who value discretion and continuity over volume and velocity.

The firm was founded in 2007 as a settlement shop for a single family office. It has since grown into a full-service desk serving forty-plus counterparties across the Gulf, South Asia and Europe — but the operating ethos has not changed. Relationships are slow; spreads are fair; mandates are narrow; reporting is meticulous.

We sit intentionally outside the retail market. Our clients are introduced, documented to LBMA and DMCC standard, and onboarded with patience. If that feels slow, it is meant to.
